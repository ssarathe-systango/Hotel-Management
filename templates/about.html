About :

This is a Hotel Management website. In this website, People can view or search the hotel for living, and the owner of hotel can upload his hotel on website for people. At the home page of website. There is a navigation bar. Where you will look three options About , Contact us and Feedback. In about you can read about hotel. In Contact us, you can contact with us by fill the form. In Feedback you can give your valuable feedback about our website. Feedback, you can give like what is the weak part , what are bugs, what can we improve in this website etc.

As a person :

If you click on as a Person, So you redirect to the login template. If you are a already member of this website as a Person so you can simply login, Otherwise you will have to do register. You will fill the registration form and After registration you redirect to the login template. Then fill the email and password and simple click on login button. If your email and password are correct then you redirect to the that template where you will look different types of hotels . You can search according to hotel name and city . If you want to see the hotel detail , Simply click on the hotel name then you redirect to the hotel detail's template. Where you can see hotel detail and simple back and then see other hotel then back and after visit ,you can logout.

As a owner :

If you click on as a owner button, you will redirect to the buttons template where you will look different types of button like home, update, show, login, register. If you are already a member of this website you can simply login Otherwise you will have to register. You will fill the registration form and After registration you redirect to the login template. Then fill the email and password and simple click on login button. If your email and password are correct then you redirect to the that template where you will upload your hotel details like hotel images , room charge , hotel name , contact number etc. After upload detail you will redirect to the buttons template again. Now you can show your hotel details by using your email , hotel email and password. After show detail you can logout and redirect to the buttons template. If you want to update the some details of your hotel you can update hotel by login through your email, hotel email and password. After update details, you will redirect to the buttons template. And you can click home button to go to the home page of website.
